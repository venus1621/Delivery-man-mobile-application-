# Privacy Policy Template for Gebeta Delivery Driver App

**Last updated**: [Current Date]

## Introduction

Gebeta Delivery ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use the Gebeta Delivery Driver mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you register and use our App, we collect:
- **Name**: Your full name
- **Email Address**: For account communication
- **Phone Number**: For order notifications and support
- **Profile Photo**: Optional, for account identification
- **Payment Information**: Bank account details for earnings transfer

### 1.2 Location Data
- **Precise Location**: GPS coordinates while the app is in use
- **Background Location**: GPS tracking during active deliveries (with your permission)
- **Location History**: Route data for completed deliveries

### 1.3 Delivery Information
- **Order History**: Details of deliveries you've completed
- **Earnings Data**: Payment records and transaction history
- **Performance Metrics**: Ratings, completion rates, and statistics
- **Proof of Delivery**: QR codes, signatures, and photos captured during deliveries

### 1.4 Device Information
- **Device ID**: Unique identifier for your device
- **Operating System**: Android version information
- **App Version**: Version of the App you're using
- **Device Model**: Type and model of your device

### 1.5 Usage Data
- **App Activity**: Features used, screens viewed, time spent
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data about app crashes
- **Performance Data**: App loading times and responsiveness

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Primary Purposes
We use your information to:
- **Provide Delivery Services**: Assign orders, calculate routes, and facilitate deliveries
- **Process Payments**: Calculate and transfer your earnings
- **Customer Tracking**: Share your real-time location with customers during active deliveries
- **Navigation**: Provide directions to pickup and delivery locations
- **Communication**: Send order notifications, updates, and support messages
- **Verification**: Confirm order pickups and deliveries using QR codes

### 2.2 Secondary Purposes
- **Performance Improvement**: Analyze delivery efficiency and app performance
- **Customer Support**: Assist with technical issues and inquiries
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms
- **Analytics**: Understand usage patterns to improve services

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 With Customers
During active deliveries, we share:
- Your first name
- Your real-time location
- Your vehicle type
- Estimated arrival time

### 3.2 With Restaurants
We share:
- Your name and phone number
- Your location when approaching pickup
- Order acceptance status

### 3.3 With Third-Party Service Providers
We may share data with:
- **Payment Processors**: For earnings transfers
- **Mapping Services** (Google Maps): For navigation
- **Cloud Services** (Firebase): For real-time data sync
- **Analytics Tools**: For app improvement
- **Customer Support Tools**: For helpdesk services

### 3.4 With Law Enforcement
We may disclose information:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or property
- In emergency situations to protect safety

### 3.5 Business Transfers
If Gebeta Delivery is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted over HTTPS

- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Location Tracking

### 7.1 Foreground Location
Used when app is open to:
- Display your position on the map
- Calculate routes to destinations
- Track delivery progress

### 7.2 Background Location
Used during active deliveries to:
- Continue tracking when app is minimized
- Update customers on your ETA
- Record delivery routes

**You can disable background location in Settings, but this will prevent you from accepting deliveries.**

### 7.3 Location Accuracy
- We use GPS for precise location (within meters)
- Location is updated every 3-5 seconds during deliveries
- Location data is encrypted during transmission
